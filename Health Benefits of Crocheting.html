Health Benefits of Crocheting

Crocheting offers a surprising array of health benefits. Beyond its calming effect on a person's mind, the repetitive motions of crochet-

ing can improve dexterity, and fine motor skills, particularly in the hands and fingers. This makes our mind able to be more relaxed and freer

from anxious ideas and thoughts. This are some of the good effect of crocheting:

• Stress relief

• Creativity

• Mental stimulation

• Physical benefits

• Practical uses
